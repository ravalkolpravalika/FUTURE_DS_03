# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Introduction

This project focuses on analyzing marketing funnel and conversion performance using customer and campaign data. The objective of the analysis is to understand how users move through different funnel stages from visitors to leads and finally to customers. The project aims to identify conversion bottlenecks, analyze customer drop-off patterns, evaluate marketing channel performance, and generate business insights that can help improve overall conversion rates and customer acquisition strategies.

## Objectives

* Analyze customer journey and funnel conversion performance
* Identify drop-off points across funnel stages
* Evaluate traffic-to-lead and lead-to-customer conversion rates
* Compare performance across channels and campaigns
* Generate business insights and recommendations to improve conversions

## Tools and Technologies Used

* Python
* Jupyter Notebook
* Tableau
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

## Dataset

The dataset used for this project was taken from Kaggle.

Dataset Link:
[Direct-to-Consumer E-commerce Funnel Dataset on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yashch05/direct-to-consumer-e-commerce-funnel-dataset?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

## Project Workflow

The project started with data cleaning and preprocessing using Python in Jupyter Notebook. Missing values, duplicate records, and data inconsistencies were handled before performing exploratory data analysis (EDA). Funnel metrics and conversion-related calculations were performed to analyze user movement across different marketing stages.

Exploratory data analysis was conducted to study traffic behavior, lead generation, customer conversion trends, marketing channel effectiveness, and campaign performance. Various visualizations were created to identify conversion bottlenecks and customer drop-off points throughout the funnel.

After completing the analysis in Python, Tableau was used to create an interactive dashboard for visual storytelling and business-focused insights. The dashboard presents conversion performance, funnel trends, marketing effectiveness, and actionable recommendations in a clear and professional format.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard includes:

* KPI cards for total visitors, total leads, total customers, conversion rates, and drop-off rates
* Funnel stage analysis
* Traffic-to-lead conversion analysis
* Lead-to-customer conversion analysis
* Marketing channel performance comparison
* Campaign-wise conversion analysis
* Customer drop-off analysis across funnel stages
* Interactive filters for channels, campaigns, and time periods

## Key Insights

* Significant customer drop-off was observed between the lead and customer conversion stages
* Certain marketing channels generated higher traffic but lower conversion rates
* High-performing campaigns showed better lead quality and customer acquisition rates
* Conversion performance varied across channels and campaign types
* Funnel optimization opportunities were identified in customer engagement and lead nurturing stages

## Recommendations

* Improve lead nurturing strategies to reduce drop-offs during conversion stages
* Focus marketing investment on high-performing channels and campaigns
* Optimize low-performing funnel stages to improve customer conversion rates
* Develop personalized engagement strategies for potential customers
* Monitor conversion KPIs regularly to improve marketing performance and customer acquisition

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how marketing funnel analysis and conversion tracking can help businesses optimize customer acquisition strategies and improve overall conversion performance. By combining Python-based analysis with Tableau visualizations, the project provides actionable insights that support data-driven marketing decisions and business growth strategies.
